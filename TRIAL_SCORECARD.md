## Scoring Guide

Strong signal:

- Worker understands layout without much explanation.
- Tool saves time or material.
- Shop asks to test another order.
- Shop discusses price or deployment.

Promising:

- Workflow is useful but one or two missing fields block daily use.

Weak:

- User likes the idea but cannot give real orders or does not feel current pain.

Not a fit:

- They already have a full system, or their workflow requires machine-specific post-processing from day one.
